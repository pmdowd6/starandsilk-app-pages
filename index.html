The app

A calmer daily read, based on your chart instead of a generic sun sign.

Star and Silk combines your natal chart with daily transits to help you understand the mood, pressure, and timing of the day. It is made for personal reflection, not noisy horoscope scrolling.

Real chart context

Birth chart, daily reading, Sky view, forecast, and saved reading history are included in the launch version.

Private by design

Your birth profile, readings, settings, and chart data are saved on this device. There is no Star and Silk account.

Free launch version

There are no in-app purchases, ads, analytics SDKs, or tracking in v1.

Inside Star and Silk

Open it once a day. Leave with one useful sentence.

The app keeps the daily ritual simple: read the thread, understand the main sky pattern, and save what you want to remember.

  1. 01Today: what to lean into, what to avoid, and why.
  2. 02My Chart: your natal context without needing a textbook.
  3. 03Sky: current transits and their plain-language meaning.
  4. 04Past: a local reading history so patterns do not disappear.

Questions

The short version.

Does Star and Silk make an account for me?

No. The launch version has no Star and Silk account system.

Does the app track me?

No third-party advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or tracking SDKs are included in v1.

Is the reading medical, legal, or financial advice?

No. Star and Silk is for personal reflection and entertainment.

Can I delete my app data?

Yes. Because launch data is stored locally, deleting the app from your device removes the app's local data.
