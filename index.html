Star and Silk

Private daily astrology from your real birth chart, built for quiet clarity and on-device use.

What It Does

Star and Silk combines your natal chart with daily transits to help you read the mood, timing, and pressure of the day. It is designed for personal reflection, not generic horoscope scrolling.

Privacy First

Your birth profile, readings, settings, and chart data are saved on your device. The app does not use accounts, ads, analytics SDKs, or tracking.

Included in v1

The launch version includes the daily reading, natal chart, Sky view, forecast, and saved reading history without an account or in-app purchase.
