Positioning

A calm astrology ritual for people who want one useful read, not another noisy feed.

Star and Silk turns a birth chart and current sky patterns into a short daily reading. The product is built around trust: no public profile, no account, no ads, and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs in v1.

Product Private daily astrology for iPhone

Daily reading, My Chart, Sky, Past readings, settings, and optional local reminders.

Audience Reflective, privacy-aware astrology users

Built for people who want calm interpretation instead of high-volume horoscope scrolling.
