Common questions

Fast answers for launch.

Is there a Star and Silk account?

No. The launch version has no account system.

Where is my chart data stored?

Your chart profile, readings, settings, and history are designed to be saved locally on your device.

Can I delete my app data?

Yes. Deleting the app removes the app's local data from your device.

What if notifications do not work?

Check iOS notification permission for Star and Silk, then email support if reminders still do not appear.

Is this professional advice?

No. Star and Silk provides astrology readings for personal reflection and entertainment.

How do I join the launch list?

Email support@starandsilk.app and ask to be notified when the App Store link is ready.
