Star and Silk Support

Need help with Star and Silk? Contact support@starandsilk.app.

What to Include

When you contact support, include:

  • Your device model
  • Your iOS version
  • The Star and Silk app version
  • A short description of what happened
  • A screenshot, if it helps explain the issue

Purchases

The launch version does not include subscriptions or in-app purchases.

Birth Time and Location

For the most accurate chart, enter your birth time and birth location as closely as possible. If you do not know your exact birth time, Star and Silk uses midday for now. Your daily reading still works, but rising sign and house details may be less precise.

Privacy

Star and Silk is designed to store your chart profile and readings on your device. Read the Privacy Policy for details.

TestFlight Feedback

If you are testing through TestFlight, you can also send feedback directly from the TestFlight app. Include what screen you were on and what you expected to happen.
