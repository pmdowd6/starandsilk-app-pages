Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026

Star and Silk is designed as a private, on-device astrology app. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information You Enter

The app may ask for birth details such as date, time, place, latitude, longitude, and time zone so it can calculate your chart and daily astrology readings.

This information is stored locally on your device. Star and Silk does not create an account for you and does not send your birth profile to an external server.

Location

If you choose to use current location during setup, the app uses that permission to help fill location coordinates for chart setup. Location is optional and is only requested when you tap Use Current Location. If you use Find City, iOS may use Apple's geocoding service to return coordinates for the city search. The app does not transmit your birth profile or location to the developer.

Purchases

The launch version does not sell subscriptions or in-app purchases.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, the app may schedule local reminders on your device. These notifications are handled by iOS. The app does not send notification data to an external server.

Analytics, Ads, and Tracking

Star and Silk does not use third-party advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or tracking SDKs. The app does not track you across apps or websites.

Data Collection Summary

Based on the current launch version, Star and Silk does not collect personal data from the app developer's server because the app has no account system, analytics backend, advertising SDK, or external profile storage.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your chart profile, readings, history, preferences, and notification settings may be stored locally on your device. You can remove app data by deleting the app from your device.

Children

Star and Silk is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if Star and Silk adds accounts, analytics, server sync, or other features that change how data is handled. The effective date will be updated when the policy changes.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@starandsilk.app.
